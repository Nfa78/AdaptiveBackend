# Garden Scene

## Setup
- Player starts in front of the stairs
- Rocky road in the immediate foreground
- Closed garden bordered by a wooden fence
- Warehouse located on the right side
- Player navigates by rotating and clicking on task stations

## Tasks
- Leaves are scattered on the walkway to the warehouse or on the house stairs. The player must either mop them or blow them away with a device.
- Water the plants near the stairs.
- Pick up fruits from the tree or the floor (final source to be decided).
- Throw seeds or fertilizer onto a small planting area on the left side of the garden.
- Straighten up a flower pot that is on the floor.
